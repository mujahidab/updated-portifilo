Beyond the Workbench

Creative Passions
& Life Interests

When I'm not designing circuits or writing code, I explore the world through these activities that fuel my creativity and drive for excellence.

Cricket

Cricket is more than just a sport to me; it's about strategy, teamwork, and persistence. I find the physics of the game fascinating and enjoy the mental discipline it requires during high-pressure moments on the field.

Touring

Exploring new roads and cultures through touring allows me to disconnect and gain fresh perspectives. The freedom of the open road and the challenge of navigating diverse terrains mirror the problem-solving nature of engineering.

Hiking & Photography

Hiking brings me closer to nature, while photography allows me to capture the intricate details of the world. Combining both helps me practice patience and attention to detail, which are essential in my technical projects.

3D Modeling & Animation

I love bringing ideas to life in the digital realm through 3D modeling. This hobby bridges the gap between my imagination and physical hardware, allowing me to prototype and refine my mechanical designs before fabrication.
